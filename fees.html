Fees

Fee

The fee for a 50 minute session is $150 including GST. Fees are payable at the time of each session.

ACC

ACC fully fund therapy for anyone that has experienced sexual abuse or assault in New Zealand. ACC support is available when you’re ready - the event could have occurred recently or several years ago and there is no requirement to have told anyone or have reported the abuse to police. As an ACC therapist my role is to navigate the healing journey alongside you. Family and whanau support is also available throughout the therapy process.

If this service seems appropriate for you or someone you care about, I encourage you to reach out and we can discuss eligibility.

Government Support and Insurance

Government agencies in New Zealand, such as Work and Income and Oranga Tamariki, may fund or subsidies psychological services. It is recommend that you discuss with your case worker to check eligibility.

Many private health insurance organisations fund a limited amount of psychological services. It is recommend you discuss with your insurer or check your policy.

Cancellation fee

If you’re unable to attend a session or require your session time to be changed, please give a minimum of 24 hours notice or a cancellation fee will apply.
