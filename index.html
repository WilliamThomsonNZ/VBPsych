Meet the Psychologist

Kia ora, I am Victoria Barendsen, a registered psychologist in the sunny Hawkes Bay. I specialise in working with children, young people and their families as they navigate the many challenges of life. I provide evidence based therapy models including EMDR, Acceptance and commitment therapy and mindfulness. I provide sexual abuse and sexual harm prevention therapy, individual therapy and a range of workshops.

I am passionate about seeing young people live their best life - a life that includes growth, curiosity and an abundance happiness. I would love to learn what you or your young person are going through and how I can support during this time.
